HOA Removal Guidelines

/Feds will review HOA removal requests that are a violation of our Terms of Service ("TOS").

Law Enforcement Request Guidelines

/Feds will respond to all law enforcement requests from authorized officials. Law Enforcement Requests Guidelines show how authorized law enforcement officials and agents can contact us to request HOA removal in accordance with applicable laws and policies. To facilitate processing, we require that all requests be addressed directly to the /Feds admin account email. Please include proper Mutual Legal Assistance Treaty documents if cross-border law enforcement is involved.
/Feds is a static site and does not retain any profile/user information that is not directly provided to /Feds such as social links, pfps etc. We cannot cooperate with data requests because we store no information that isn't public. We will still grant information access we have if accompanied by a valid court order. We will voluntarily cooperate with law enforcement requests from all jurisdictions for HOA removal requests when the content violates our TOS. All law enforcement requests should be submitted via email to admin@fedsgov.shop for processing.
