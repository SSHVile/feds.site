Information We Collect

/Feds collects only information that users voluntarily submit for public display on their profiles. This may include profile images, banners, music selections, usernames, and social links.
/Feds does not collect private personal data, real names, IP addresses, precise locations, device identifiers, behavioral analytics, tracking cookies, or background activity.

How Information Is Used

Submitted information is used solely to render and display user profiles and platform content. /Feds does not sell, rent, trade, or monetize user information and does not use data for advertising, tracking, or profiling.

Data Storage & Retention

/Feds stores only the content that users explicitly choose to make public. No hidden logs, access records, or historical metadata are retained beyond what is required to operate the site.

Third Parties

/Feds does not share user information with third parties, advertisers, analytics providers, or data brokers. External links are governed by the privacy policies of their respective platforms.

Law Enforcement Requests

/Feds responds only to lawful requests from authorized law enforcement agencies when accompanied by valid legal documentation. Because /Feds does not retain private user data, we can only provide information that is publicly available or voluntarily submitted.
Requests must be submitted to admin@fedgov.shop.

User Control & Rights

Users maintain control over the content they submit and may edit or remove their profile information at any time. /Feds does not track user activity beyond the content users choose to publish.

Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of /Feds constitutes acceptance of any revisions.
